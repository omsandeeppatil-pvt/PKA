A Firm Built on Precision and Professional Integrity

Paritosh Khandelwal & Associates is a peer-reviewed firm of Company Secretaries, providing legal, secretarial, compliance and strategic advisory services to businesses across industries.

The firm specialises in Company Law, Corporate Law and Business Management consultancy, with deep expertise across Company Formation, ROC Compliances, Secretarial Audits, Corporate Governance and Regulatory Advisory. Registered with ICSI, the firm is committed to professional integrity, technical excellence and ethical practice.

Vision

To be the trusted legal and compliance advisor, offering reliable, value-driven corporate governance solutions that support sustainable business growth.

Mission

To deliver reliable secretarial, legal and advisory services that uphold the highest standards of corporate governance.

02 — Secretarial Services

ROC Filings, Registers & Ongoing Compliance

The operational backbone of your compliance programme — from MCA filings and event-based returns to statutory registers, resolutions and share transfer compliances.

02
01
ROC & MCA Filings

Preparation and filing of statutory forms, returns and disclosures with the Registrar of Companies through the MCA portal, ensuring compliance with corporate laws and regulatory requirements.

02
Annual Compliance Management

Managing yearly compliance requirements of companies and LLPs, including annual filings, statutory disclosures and regulatory documentation within prescribed timelines.

03
Event-Based Compliances

Handling compliance filings and documentation arising from specific corporate events such as change in directors, allotment of shares, increase in authorised capital or change in registered office.

04
Statutory Registers & Records

Maintaining statutory registers, records and corporate documents as mandated under the Companies Act to ensure proper governance and regulatory preparedness at all times.

05
Drafting Resolutions & Corporate Documents

Drafting board resolutions, shareholder resolutions, notices, minutes, agreements and other corporate documents required for legal and procedural compliance.

06
Share Allotment & Transfer Compliance

Managing legal and procedural compliances related to issue, allotment, transfer, transmission and dematerialisation of shares in accordance with applicable corporate laws and regulations.
