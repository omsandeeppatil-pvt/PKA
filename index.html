Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions

What compliance services do you provide?
We provide end-to-end corporate compliance services including ROC filings, secretarial audits, corporate governance advisory, company incorporation, share management and regulatory representation across various authorities.
Can you help with company incorporation?
Yes. We manage the complete incorporation process for private limited companies, public companies and LLPs, including name approval, documentation, MOA and AOA drafting, and post-incorporation compliance.
Do you assist startups?
We work closely with startups and early-stage businesses, helping them set up the right corporate structure, stay compliant through funding rounds, and build governance practices that scale with growth.
Do you provide legal advisory?
Our legal advisory covers corporate law, contract drafting and review, regulatory interpretation, and representation before authorities such as ROC, RBI and SEBI.
How can we contact your firm?
You can reach us through the contact form below, call us directly, or email us. Our office is located at Parmar Chambers, Sadhu Vaswani Chowk, Pune.
