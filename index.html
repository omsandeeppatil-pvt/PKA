A Firm Built on Precision and Professional Integrity

Paritosh Khandelwal & Associates is a company secretarial and corporate compliance practice based in Pune, serving businesses across India with specialist knowledge in company law, governance, and regulatory affairs.

The firm was established to provide structured, dependable secretarial and compliance support to businesses at every stage — from incorporation through to complex corporate transactions. Our practice is grounded in an understanding that compliance is not merely procedural; it is foundational to the long-term health and credibility of any business.

We serve startups, closely held private companies, and established enterprises, offering continuity of advice and hands-on support across all significant corporate events and regulatory requirements.

Our Services

Professional Services for Every Stage of Your Business

Company Incorporation

End-to-end incorporation services for private limited companies, LLPs, OPCs, and Section 8 companies, including name reservation, documentation, and post-incorporation compliance.

ROC Compliance

Preparation and filing of statutory returns, annual filings, event-based forms, and maintenance of statutory registers in accordance with the Companies Act, 2013.

Corporate Governance

Board secretarial support, meeting management, agenda preparation, minutes drafting, and advisory on board composition and committee governance.

Secretarial Audit

Independent secretarial audit under Section 204 of the Companies Act for prescribed companies, providing assurance on compliance with applicable laws, rules, and regulations.

Corporate Legal Advisory

Legal drafting, vetting, and advisory on corporate documents including shareholder agreements, board resolutions, and statutory filings requiring legal precision.

Share Capital & Securities

Advisory and compliance for share issuance, transfer, transmission, buyback, and private placement, including maintenance of share records and the register of members.

Mergers & Amalgamations

Secretarial and compliance support for mergers, demergers, amalgamations, and acquisitions, including drafting of schemes, filing, and regulatory liaison.

Regulatory Representation

Representation before the Registrar of Companies, National Company Law Tribunal, and other regulatory bodies on matters of statutory compliance and corporate law.
